BIO

Sheila Nduhukire is an accomplished multi-media practitioner and content strategist with 6 years of journalism experience in Uganda and Kenya. She holds an Executive MBA and is pursuing a MA International Public Relations and Global Communications Management.

Sheila is a member of The Chartered Institute of Public Relations (CIPR), Europe's largest membership organization of PR professionals.

Sheila is passionate about #technology and uses data Analytics and customer engagement to deliver insight based campaigns and communications.

She is self-motivated with excellent written and interpersonal skills . She has deep understanding of global issues particularly Africa and Europe. As a political and investigative journalist, Sheila covered the Ugandan parliament and politics in the Great Lakes Region. She’s skilled in using social listening tools and data analytics to interpret user behavior and break down large data sets.
